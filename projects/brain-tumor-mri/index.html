MEDICAL IMAGING · PROJECT 03

Brain Tumor Detection & Segmentation

A two-stage MRI workflow combining scan-level detection with pixel-level tumor segmentation and reviewable visual overlays.

MRIResNetResUNet7,800 scansGitHub ↗

From classification to location

The approach

The project used ResNet for tumor detection and ResUNet for segmentation across a dataset of 7,800 MRI scans. Detection reached 92% accuracy.

  • Prepared MRI inputs for repeatable model training and evaluation.
  • Used a ResNet classifier to detect the presence of a tumor.
  • Used ResUNet to localize tumor regions at pixel level.
  • Produced overlays that preserve the original scan context for review.

Portfolio interpretation

The project demonstrates an end-to-end medical imaging pattern: detect an abnormality, localize it, and communicate the result in a form a reviewer can inspect.

Verified headline result. Dataset size, architectures, and 92% detection accuracy are sourced from the CV. Split design, segmentation metrics, and qualitative samples remain pending the original project artifacts.
