The evaluation gap

A high frame score can hide a bad surgical sequence.

Aggregate scores can hide flicker, late transitions, and confident errors.

01

Prediction flicker

Frame labels can oscillate rapidly even when aggregate accuracy appears acceptable.

02

Boundary errors

Late or premature phase transitions matter operationally, but disappear inside frame averages.

03
92%

Confident mistakes

A wrong answer delivered with high certainty is precisely where human review becomes valuable.

Research foundations

Built on the work that moved surgical video understanding forward.

OR Lens is an independent implementation and evaluation project. These primary papers inform its dataset choice, temporal metrics, model roadmap, and definition of reliable phase recognition.

DATASET + BASELINE2016

EndoNet: A Deep Architecture for Recognition Tasks on Laparoscopic Videos

Twinanda et al. Introduced the Cholec80 benchmark and a joint tool-presence and surgical-phase recognition architecture.

IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging↗
TEMPORAL MODELLING2020

TeCNO: Surgical Phase Recognition with Multi-Stage Temporal Convolutional Networks

Czempiel et al. demonstrated how multi-stage temporal modelling improves phase recognition beyond isolated frame classification.

International Journal of CARS↗
LONG-RANGE CONTEXT2023

LoViT: Long Video Transformer for Surgical Phase Recognition

Combines short- and long-range temporal information for online recognition across long surgical procedures.

arXiv:2305.08989↗
ONLINE RECOGNITION2023

SKiT: A Fast Key Information Video Transformer for Online Surgical Phase Recognition

Liu et al. introduced key-information memory for efficient online reasoning over long surgical procedures.

ICCV · CVF Open Access↗
FOUNDATION MODEL2024

GSViT: General Surgery Vision Transformer

Explores surgical video pre-training at scale and motivates the roadmap toward verified, architecture-faithful model adapters.

arXiv:2403.05949↗
HIERARCHICAL ATTENTION2024

Surgformer: Surgical Transformer with Hierarchical Temporal Attention

Uses multi-resolution temporal aggregation to capture global and local context in surgical phase recognition.

MICCAI Proceedings↗

Why this matters to a startup

Evaluation infrastructure is product infrastructure.

For surgical AI teams, the path from a good model to a trustworthy workflow runs through repeatable evidence and efficient failure review.

01

Faster model comparison

One schema for predictions, temporal behaviour, calibration, and latency.

02

Focused clinical review

Surface the segments that deserve expert attention instead of replaying entire procedures.

03

Evidence for iteration

Turn failure patterns into concrete data, model, and workflow improvements.

04

Reproducible reporting

Keep dataset splits, runtime metadata, predictions, and reports auditable.
